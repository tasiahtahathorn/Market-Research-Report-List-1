<p><p>Thermoelectric Generators (TEGs) convert heat directly into electricity using thermoelectric materials. The market for TEGs is categorized into single-stage and multi-stage systems. Single-stage TEGs operate optimally over a limited temperature range, making them suitable for specific applications like small-scale power generation. Multi-stage TEGs, on the other hand, employ stacked thermoelectric materials to capture a broader temperature gradient, enhancing efficiency and power output, which is ideal for industrial applications where higher energy conversion is needed.</p></p>
