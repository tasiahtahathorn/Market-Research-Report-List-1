<p><p>The Thin Light Box market encompasses three primary types of lighting technologies: LED, EEFL (External Electrode Fluorescent Lamp), and T4 fluorescent bulbs. LED panels are energy-efficient and long-lasting, making them increasingly popular for modern designs. EEFLs offer higher brightness and efficiency than standard fluorescent options, ideal for larger displays. T4 fluorescent bulbs are compact and provide good color rendering, often used in applications with limited space. Each type caters to specific requirements in lighting performance, energy consumption, and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
