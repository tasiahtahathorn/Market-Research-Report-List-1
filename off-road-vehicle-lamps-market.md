<p><ul><li>Halogen</li><li>HID</li><li>LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The off-road vehicle lamps market is primarily segmented into three types: halogen, HID (High-Intensity Discharge), and LED (Light Emitting Diode). Halogen lamps are affordable and provide warm light but consume more energy. HID lamps are brighter and more energy-efficient than halogen, making them suitable for off-roading. LED lamps offer the best longevity, durability, and energy efficiency, with a wide range of brightness levels and color temperatures, making them popular for modern off-road vehicles. Each type caters to specific performance and aesthetic preferences.</p></p>
