<p><ul><li>Diagonal Tires</li><li>Radial Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck tire market features two primary types: diagonal (bias) tires and radial tires. Diagonal tires have layers of fabric that crisscross, providing durability and resistance to punctures, making them suitable for off-road conditions. Conversely, radial tires have a steel belt that runs circumferentially, offering enhanced flexibility, better traction, and improved fuel efficiency on highways. While diagonal tires are often favored for specific applications, radial tires dominate the long-haul trucking sector due to their superior performance and longevity.</p></p>
