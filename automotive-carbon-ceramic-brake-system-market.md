<p><p>The Automotive Carbon Ceramic Brake System market features two primary types: single disc brake systems and multiple disc brake systems. Single disc brake systems utilize one carbon ceramic disc per wheel, offering lightweight efficiency and improved performance, making them ideal for sports cars. In contrast, multiple disc brake systems integrate several discs, enhancing braking power and thermal management, suitable for high-performance vehicles and motorsport applications. Both systems leverage carbon ceramic technology for superior heat resistance and longevity, optimizing overall braking performance.</p></p>
