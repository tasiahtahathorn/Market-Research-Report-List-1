<p><strong>The Elastomer Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Neoprene Bearing</li><li>Laminated Elastic Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elastomer bearing market comprises mainly two types: neoprene bearings and laminated elastic bearings. Neoprene bearings are made from synthetic rubber, providing excellent resistance to weathering, chemicals, and oils, making them suitable for various civil engineering applications like bridges. On the other hand, laminated elastic bearings consist of multiple layers of rubber and steel, offering superior flexibility and load distribution, which enhances structural performance. Both types are crucial for accommodating movements and reducing vibrations in construction and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
