<p><p>The laminated steel container market is primarily divided into two types: 2-piece cans and 3-piece cans. 2-piece cans are manufactured from a single sheet of metal, resulting in a seamless design that enhances durability and reduces leakage. In contrast, 3-piece cans are constructed from three separate components: two cylindrical bodies and a lid, often offering more flexibility in size and shape. Both types serve essential functions in packaging food, beverages, and chemicals, promoting product safety and preservation.</p></p>
